NOV 2021 Â· Academic/personal project

Banco Alimentar is a portuguese social solidarity institution whose great mission is to fight against food waste and hunger.

The aim of the project was to give Banco Alimentar a new identity, working with the values of it's mission and its remarkable name.

Reflecting on BA's graphic history, the blue was taken as an obvious choice. As well it's also able to reenforce a trustworthiness and stability, attributed to the color.

The new identity was applied on the construction of an user interface for the institution's app.

It's creation falls under practical purposes such as giving recent news, information of the institution and its local branches, volunteering data, donations and others.

Through the font and the created icons and illustrative details the whole gains an approachable and easy going feel.

The main goal here was to create a clean interface that provides the user essential information in a simple and effective way.
