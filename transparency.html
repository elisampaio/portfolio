Transparency

Web Design

OCT 2021 Â· Academic project

Having the terms 'Transparency' and 'Privacy' as a motto, this projects aims to represent the relation of the two through a visual poetry approach.

Early on the process, the initial interface designs already reflected the desire for a clean and modern feel.

The simplicity of the interface contrasts the intricacies of the textual elements of the piece, leaving enough room for it to claim the spotlight.

Taking advantage of variable fonts, the animation of all the textual elements is made through the manipulation of the fonts axis.

The whole piece reflects an attention to detail, taking care of every component present.
